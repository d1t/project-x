What are the fees associated with investing on Figg?
Joining Figg is free – there is no fee associated with being a member of our investor community. When you make an investment, you will be charged a processing fee, which is predominantly used to cover payment processing costs, so this may vary depending on your location, but there are no annual fees for the ongoing support we provide once you’ve made an investment.

The minimum you can invest in any one company is $10 or the equivalent in your local currency.

Risk warning


Investing in early stage businesses involves risk as they are an illiquid asset class, which do not pay dividends and can only be liquidated during an exit event like an acquisition or a public listing. Figg is targeted at financial backers who are adequately sophisticated to understand these risks and make their own investment decisions. You will only be able to invest through Figg once you are registered as adequately sophisticated.

While investing in startups or early stage businesses can be extremely lucrative, they also have a high failure rate and any investment in these businesses should be part of a wider portfolio diversification strategy. Read the full Risk Warning
