Idea

Our mission at Figg is to enable anyone, anywhere in the world the ability to invest in startups in Africa. To achieve this, we are starting by lowering the barriers to participating in early stage rounds by building processes and systems that provides entrepreneurs and teams with the capital they need in a simple and effecient manner.

Figg was built to answer a question - what if all the right People, Resources and Capital came together for Africa to Bloom? We aim to connect the world's capital with the teams solving some of Africa's problems in an innovative and scalable way. We are betting that people will come together with resources, capital and expertise for the development of the continent.

Risk warning


Investing in early stage businesses involves risk as they are an illiquid asset class, which do not pay dividends and can only be liquidated during an exit event like an acquisition or a public listing. Figg is targeted at financial backers who are adequately sophisticated to understand these risks and make their own investment decisions. You will only be able to invest through Figg once you are registered as adequately sophisticated. While investing in startups or early stage businesses can be extremely lucrative, they also have a high failure rate and any investment in these businesses should be part of a wider portfolio diversification strategy.
